Click The Button For Help! This is a turn based dice game where each player will roll their dice which will return the value of their attack power. After each player rolls, the attack button must be pressed for an attack to occur. The attack button will determine which player has the winning roll and subtract the winning roll by the losing roll which is the value that the winning player attacks with. After an attack happens the losing players health will be subtracted by the value of the attack. These steps will be repeated until one of the players health reaches 0. The player who's health stays above 0 is the winner.
