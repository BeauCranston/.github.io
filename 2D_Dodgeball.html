Overview

2D Dodgeball is a game that aims to bring gym class to the 2D platform! A game for all of the not-so athletic to take revenge on the jocks that embarrased them in real life dodgeball. The game is simple, 2 Players, 1 court, 2 ball spitting pipes on each side, and all of the chaotic dodgeball fun you could ask for. The game starts with one ball in the middle of the court which the players race to grab, the users will toss the ball back and forth trying to hit each other with it and get points. You can get points by either hitting the other player with a ball, or by catching a ball that the enemy player throws at you, first to five points declares the winner. Other game modes will be included along with the classic game mode such as a 2v2 team based dodgeball game where you must knock the two players out however, both teams have the ability to revive their partner when their partner goes down, and the other game mode will be a practice mode where the user can practice catching the balls, throwing the balls off walls and mastering different angles, and trying to master other skills that come with being a dodgeball master.
