Overview

2D Dodgeball is a game that aims to bring gym class to the 2D platform! A game for all of the not-so athletic to take revenge on the jocks that embarrased them in real life dodgeball. The game is simple, 2 Players, 1 court, 2 ball spitting pipes on each side, and all of the chaotic dodgeball fun you could ask for. The game starts with one ball in the middle of the court which the players race to grab, the users will toss the ball back and forth trying to hit each other with it and get points. You can get points by either hitting the other player with a ball, or by catching a ball that the enemy player throws at you, first to five points declares the winner. Other game modes will be included along with the classic game mode such as a 2v2 team based dodgeball game where you must knock the two players out however, both teams have the ability to revive their partner when their partner goes down, and the other game mode will be a practice mode where the user can practice catching the balls, throwing the balls off walls and mastering different angles, and trying to master other skills that come with being a dodgeball master.

2V2 Mode

Help I got hit! Revive me!



The 2v2 game mode will be played a little bit differently from the original. Since there are two players it will be set up so that when a player gets hit the player is knocked down, and their teammate will be able to revive them. This can happen as long as one teammate is alive making this game mode more of a survival feeling mode with high intensity. Once both players are knocked down the opposing team wins since nobody can revive the players. Players will be able to pull off certain tasks that would not be possible in the regular game mode such as being able to pass the ball to a teammate however the teammate must catch the ball just like if it was being thrown at the player from an enemy. This allows for more of a skill cap in team mode and requires the players to think about what how they coordinate and are more involved in the teamwork aspect of the game.

Classic Mode

Pure 1V1 Action



The classic game mode is a 1 v 1 match up that will test the users wits and reaction time by trying to out play the opponent with fast paced dodgeball gameplay. The game starts with one ball on the floor and as the game goes on more and more balls are launched onto the court from the cannons. The way in which balls are added to the court is completely randomized. The canons will fire at a random angle with randomized power, and it will come from a randomized cannon. This will make the game increasingly chaotic and skill based since there is no cooldown in which players can throw the ball.
